Welcome

Welcome to the Nanomaterials for Energy and Sustainable Technologies (NEST) Research Group. Our research focuses on ion-beam modification of nanomaterials, plasmonic systems, and advanced functional materials for energy and extreme environments.

Research Interests

Ion irradiation induced defect engineering, embedded metallic nanoparticles, high entropy alloys, plasmon resonance engineering, synchrotron-based characterization, and optical response of nanocomposite systems.

Skills & Expertise

Transmission Electron Microscopy, Ion Beam Irradiation, GISAXS/GIWAXS (DESY P03), XPS, XRD, UV-Vis spectroscopy, RBS, SRIM/TRIM simulation, Python-based modelling.
