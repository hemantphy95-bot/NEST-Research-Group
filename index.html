About the NEST Research Group

Welcome to the Nanomaterials for Energy and Sustainable Technologies (NEST) Research Group. Our research focuses on the synthesis, modification, and structural evolution of advanced nanomaterials, functional materials, and complex alloy systems for energy and extreme environment applications.
